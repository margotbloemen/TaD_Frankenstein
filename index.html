Frankenstein Digital Edition

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley's Frankenstein. This project presents the transcription and visualization of Chapter 7A of the manuscript. Created by Margot Bloemen, as part of the Text as Data course, in the master Digital Text Analysis, at the University of Antwerp.
